About Me

Behind the Lens & Edit Desk

I'm Abel Leta, ​I am a Cinematographer and Video Editor with over 5 years of experience bringing creative visions to life. My journey has allowed me to collaborate with renowned production houses such as Zebra Studio, Tesfa Michael Studio, Glory Entertainment, Maya Studio, and Manna Studio. In addition to my studio work, I partner with various digital content creators and YouTubers, filming and editing engaging videos, podcasts, and short films. I specialize in combining technical precision with strong storytelling to create visually compelling content. If you're looking for professional services for your upcoming event or production, feel free to reach out.

Whether shooting on-set with professional cinema rigs or diving deep into post-production workflows, I bridge the gap between creative vision and technical execution.

Services

What I Can Do For You

Providing comprehensive production and post-production solutions to scale your brand and tell your story.

Professional Editing

Seamless transitions, narrative pacing, rhythm, dynamic cuts, and visual effects built around custom stories. Optimized for cinematic output, web series, and YouTube formats.

Color Grading

Industry-grade color correction and grading using DaVinci Resolve. Adjusting exposures, building custom LUT paths, color matching multi-cam shoots, and shaping cinematic atmospheres.

Cinematography

Professional camera operations and creative direction. Experienced with RED, ARRI, Sony, and Blackmagic rigs to capture visually stunning frames, lighting setups, and camera moves.

Sound Design

Enhancing videos with multi-layered audio landscapes. Audio cleaning, sound effects (SFX) implementation, ambient layering, and mixing that elevates the emotional weight of your video.
